Effect

Example

margin:

-top -right -bottom -left || 0 auto

Margins are used to create space around elements, outside of any defined borders.

margin: 25px 50px 75px 100px;
margin: 0 auto;

padding:

-top -right -bottom -left

Padding is used to create space around an element's content, inside of any defined borders.

padding: 25px 50px 75px 100px;

border:

-width -style -color

Border properties allow you to specify the style, width, and color of an element's border.

border: 5px solid red;

border-style:

solid || dotted || dashed || groove || double || inset || outset || none

Defines a border-style

border-style: dotted;

border-radius:

topleft -topright -botright - botleft

Sets the border a radius

border-radius: 20px;
